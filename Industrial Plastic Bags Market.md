**3. Market Segmentation**

**By Product Type**

- **Flat Bags**: Simple, open-ended bags suitable for packaging flat items.
- **Gusseted Bags**: Expandable bags that provide extra capacity, ideal for bulky items.
- **Trash Bags**: Heavy-duty bags used for waste disposal in industrial settings.
- **Woven Sacks**: Durable bags made from woven polypropylene, used for heavy-duty applications.
- **Valve Bags**: Bags with a valve for filling powders and granular materials.
- **Others**: Includes specialty bags designed for specific industrial needs.

**By Material**

- **Polyethylene (PE)**: Dominates the market due to its flexibility, moisture resistance, and cost-effectiveness.
- **Polypropylene (PP)**: Known for its strength and resistance to chemicals, used in heavy-duty applications.
- **Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC)**: Offers clarity and rigidity, suitable for specific packaging needs.
- **Biodegradable Plastics**: Gaining popularity due to environmental concerns, made from renewable resources.
- **Recycled Plastics**: Increasingly used to promote sustainability and reduce environmental impact.

**By Capacity**

- **Less than 20 kg**: Used for lightweight products and retail packaging.
- **20–50 kg**: The most common size, suitable for bulk packaging in agriculture and chemicals.
- **Above 50 kg**: Heavy-duty bags used for construction materials and large-scale industrial applications.

- **Food & Beverage**: Requires packaging that ensures product safety and extends shelf life.
- **Agriculture**: Bags used for storing and transporting grains, seeds, and fertilizers.
