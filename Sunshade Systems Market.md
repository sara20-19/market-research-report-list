**Type Segmentation**

The market is primarily segmented into the following types:

- **Fixed Sunshade Systems**: These are stationary structures designed to provide permanent shading solutions. They are commonly used in commercial and residential buildings to reduce solar heat gain and enhance indoor comfort.​
- **Adjustable Sunshade Systems**: These systems offer flexibility by allowing users to adjust the angle and position of the shading elements. They are suitable for applications where sun exposure varies throughout the day.​
- **Motorized Sunshade Systems**: Equipped with automated mechanisms, these systems can be controlled remotely or programmed to adjust based on environmental conditions. They are increasingly popular in smart homes and commercial buildings.​
